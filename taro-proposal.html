01

Trip-Based Matching

Match people heading to the same destination. Both going to Moab next week? You rank higher in each other's daily matches. No other app does this.

02

Interest-Based Friendships

Select interests at onboarding ‚Äî climbing, surfing, yoga, photography. Find people doing the same things, heading to the same places. Not just proximity.

Three chat types: direct messages, city chats (everyone in Portland now), destination chats (everyone heading to Big Sur). Mirrors how van lifers actually communicate.

Van breaks down in Flagstaff? Browse verified builders by specialty ‚Äî electrical, plumbing, mechanical ‚Äî with real ratings and hourly rates. Hire directly in-app.

Onboarding collects gender identity and match preference. The algorithm only shows you people you want to meet. Safety and intention built-in from day one.

Daily match batch of 5. Swipe-based with shared destination context shown on each card. "Both heading to Zion in 3 days." Urgency built in naturally.

Monetization

Two revenue streams.
One live. Two planned.

The subscription model was designed from real user research ‚Äî not guesswork. I paid for TimeLeft and NomadTable specifically to test whether a hard paywall upfront changes user behavior. It does. People who commit financially show up more, engage more, and stay longer.

Hard paywall via RevenueCat before accessing matching, messaging, and full community features. Filters for serious users. Proven to increase retention and event attendance in comparable apps.
